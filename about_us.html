ABOUT US 
 A non-profit site that offers you books suitable for all age groups where you can take a picture of
       your book that you have finished reading and share it with others, request a book from a reader, 
       or search for a specific book that you want to read for free (if there is an online version of that book).
        You can also read reviews Others about the books they have read.Users are the main element in developing 
        and improving the site by adding new books and exchanging them with others. We hope that you like what we offer,
         and we hope that you will kindly develop the site and share with us 
        any improvement that leads to better ..
